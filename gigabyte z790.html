Elevate Your Gaming Experience

The GIGABYTE Z790 Gaming X AX motherboard is engineered for gamers and PC enthusiasts seeking top-tier performance and cutting-edge features. Designed to support Intel's latest processors and equipped with advanced connectivity options, this motherboard is your gateway to a high-performance gaming rig.

Why Choose the GIGABYTE Z790 Gaming X AX?

This motherboard combines robust power delivery, advanced thermal design, and next-generation connectivity to meet the demands of modern gaming and content creation. Whether you're building a new system or upgrading your current setup, the Z790 Gaming X AX offers the features and performance needed to stay ahead of the curve.

Key Features:

  • CPU Support: Compatible with Intel 12th and 13th Gen Core, Pentium Gold, and Celeron processors using the LGA 1700 socket .
  • Memory: Supports up to 192GB of DDR5 memory across 4 DIMM slots, with speeds up to 7600 MT/s .
  • Storage: Features 4 M.2 slots (PCIe 4.0 x4) and 6 SATA III ports, providing ample storage options for high-speed SSDs and traditional hard drives .
  • Expansion Slots: Equipped with 1 PCIe 5.0 x16 slot for the latest graphics cards, 1 PCIe 4.0 x16 (x4 mode), and 1 PCIe 3.0 x16 (x4 mode) for additional expansion cards .
  • Networking: Integrated Intel Wi-Fi 6E (802.11ax) for ultra-fast wireless connectivity and Realtek 2.5GbE LAN for high-speed wired networking .
  • Audio: High-definition audio with support for 7.1-channel surround sound, ensuring immersive gaming and media experiences.
  • USB Connectivity: Offers a range of USB ports, including USB 3.2 Gen 2x2 Type-C, USB 3.2 Gen 2 Type-A, USB 3.2 Gen 1, and USB 2.0 ports for versatile peripheral connections .
  • Display Outputs: Includes HDMI 2.0 and DisplayPort outputs, supporting multi-monitor setups and high-resolution displays.

Advanced Features:

  • Q-Flash Plus: Update BIOS without installing CPU, memory, or graphics card.
  • EZ-Latch Plus: Tool-free design for easy installation of M.2 SSDs.
  • Smart Fan 6: Multiple temperature sensors and hybrid fan headers for optimal cooling performance .

Form Factor: ATX (30.5cm x 24.4cm), suitable for a wide range of PC cases.
