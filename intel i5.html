Unlock elite performance without breaking the bank. The Intel Core i5-14600KF is a powerhouse designed for gamers, creators, and multitaskers who demand high-speed processing and efficiency.

Key Specifications:

  • Architecture: 14th Gen Raptor Lake Refresh
  • Cores/Threads: 14 Cores (6 Performance + 8 Efficient) / 20 Threads
  • Base Clock: 3.5 GHz (Performance Cores)
  • Max Turbo Frequency: Up to 5.3 GHz
  • Cache: 24 MB Intel® Smart Cache​
  • Socket Compatibility: LGA 1700​
  • Memory Support: DDR4-3200 / DDR5-5600, Dual Channel, up to 192 GB​
  • PCIe Support: PCIe 4.0 & 5.0​
  • Integrated Graphics: None (Discrete GPU required)
  • Thermal Design Power (TDP): 125W (Base), 181W (Maximum Turbo)​
